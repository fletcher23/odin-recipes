Pasta Blanco

Ingredients

  • 2oz Spaghetti Noodles
  • 1 cup Bone Broth
  • 3/4 cup Whole Milk
  • .5 tsp Black Pepper (at least)
  • 1 tsp Crushed Red Pepper

Instructions

  1. Pour the bone broth and milk into a pan and add pasta. Season with pepper and red pepper flakes. Cook until pasta is tender.
  2. Serve with ground beef and/or parmesean cheese.
