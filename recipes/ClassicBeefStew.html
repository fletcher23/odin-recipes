Classic Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • 2 small cans of Campbells cream of mushroom (don't add water)
  • 1 packet Lipton Beefy Onion Soup
  • 2.5 lbs of stew meat (beef)
  • .2.5 large potatoes
  • 3 carrots, peeled, chopped
  • 2-3 thin stalks of celery
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Pout contents of pan into crock pot and stir.
  2. Sear meat on high in frying pan. Olive oil or other. Save pan and charring.
  3. Place meat in crock pot and cover.
  4. Pour cream of mushroom, dry soup mix, and 1.5 cups water into frying pan and heat while stirring. Gently scrape pan while stirring to mix beef sear.
  5. Set crockpot to low and let sit for 3 hours
  6. Chop potatoes into medium sized cubes, carrots and celery into small slices. Put in prep bowl.
  7. At 3 hours mark, Put carrots, potatoes, and celery into crock pot. Stir.
  8. Set to low for another 4 hours.
  9. Noms
